- 参与者：从调用链中提取的类/服务
- 消息：方法调用（含方法名和参数）
- 示例格式：
```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant C as OrderController
    participant S as OrderService
    participant M as OrderMapper
    C->>S: create(OrderDTO)
    S->>M: insert(Order)
    M-->>S: orderId
    S-->>C: Result.ok(orderId)
```
